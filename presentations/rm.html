The process

Daily workflow

  • Upto 2 hours daily before the morning call working through the PQA queue
    • Assess the security queue
    • Assess, test and push bugs
    • Assess, test and push enhancements
    • Assess, test and push new features
    • Notify RMaints of PQA bugs that only affect their branches
    • Monitor Jenkins build for errors

Other commitments

  • Bug shepherding
  • Liase frequently with QA team
  • Chairing community meetings
  • Reaching out to parties to encourage collaboration
